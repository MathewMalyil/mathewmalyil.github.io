Privacy Policy – RoomScannerAR

Effective Date: 10 February 2026
Contact Email: malyilmathew@gmail.com

RoomScannerAR (“the App”) is developed by Mathew Malyil (“we”, “us”, “our”). This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

1) Camera (AR Scanning)

The App requests access to the device camera to enable AR-based room scanning and measurement.

  • Camera input is used only while the App is active and you are using scanning features.
  • We do not record, store, or upload camera images or video to our servers.
  • Camera processing occurs on your device to support AR measurement.

2) Vibration

The App uses vibration (haptic feedback) to enhance user experience (for example, confirmations during actions such as taps or measurement interactions). The vibration permission does not access personal data.

3) Files, Exports, and Sharing

RoomScannerAR may allow you to generate and preview documents (such as project summaries or PDFs) and optionally share/export them using apps on your device (e.g., email, messaging, cloud drives).

  • Exporting/sharing happens only when you choose to do so.
  • The App uses Android’s secure sharing mechanism to share files with other apps only on your request.
  • We do not automatically upload your documents to any server.

4) Data We Do Not Collect

Based on the current version of the App, we do not collect, store, or share:

  • Location data (GPS or network-based)
  • Names, email addresses, phone numbers, contacts
  • Microphone/audio
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Analytics or tracking data

5) Third-Party Services

RoomScannerAR uses Google ARCore / Google Play Services for AR to enable AR functionality on supported devices. We do not send personal data to third parties. If the App adds analytics, ads, cloud sync, or other third-party SDKs in the future, this policy will be updated before those features are enabled.

6) Security

Because RoomScannerAR does not require account login and does not upload personal data to our servers, your information remains primarily on your device unless you choose to export/share.

7) Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since the App does not collect personal data, it is suitable for general audiences. Parents/guardians should supervise device usage where appropriate.

8) Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
